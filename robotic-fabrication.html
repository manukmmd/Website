Robotic fabrication

KUKA robotic milling, foam forms and polymer spray finishing.

Large-format robotic fabrication from Canberra for film props, scenic builds, sculptural installations, prototypes and oversized custom shapes.

Canberra capability

Turn CAD files and foam blocks into finished physical forms.

MM Design can take a digital model through toolpath planning, robotic foam milling, hard coating, polymer spray finishing, sanding and paint-ready preparation.

This is suited to jobs where hand-building would be slow, inconsistent or too hard to scale, including film set elements, large props, sculptures, display pieces and prototype forms.
